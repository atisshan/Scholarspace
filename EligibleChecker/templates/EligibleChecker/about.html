Who We Are

ScholarSpace is a cutting-edge platform founded by Kenyan education enthusiasts to bridge the information gap between students and higher learning institutions. Born out of the frustration we experienced during our own university search, we've built a comprehensive solution that makes program discovery effortless.

Our Mission

To simplify university program discovery by providing comprehensive, accurate, and up-to-date information that helps students make informed decisions about their academic future.
